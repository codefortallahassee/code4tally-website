About Code For Tallahassee

We are a relatively new Code For America brigade located in Tallahassee, Florida; membership is free and open to the public. As local community volunteers we strive to make government work better; our goal is to open, improve, and engage local governments and community outreach programs within the Tallahasee area to pursue effective, sustainable technology solutions that solve real problems. If you can write code we need you, but we also need innovators, motivators, community organizers, business professionals, legal researchers, bloggers/marketers, technology/gadget aficionados, software/usability testers, educators/trainers, artists/designers, students, government employees, and people with non-profit experience; regardless of your indivdual skills and talents, if you're a U.S. citizen with a passion for solving community problems, we need you!
